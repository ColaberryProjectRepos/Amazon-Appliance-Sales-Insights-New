Adding Logos to the dashboard 16

To add the Colaberry and Amazon logos to your Power BI dashboard, follow these steps:
1. Go to the "Home" tab in the Power BI ribbon and click on the "Image" icon. This will allow you to insert an image onto your report canvas.
2. Choose Logo Image: A dialog box will appear, prompting you to select an image. Browse your computer and select the Colaberry logo image from the folder you downloaded earlier.
3. With the logo selected, you'll see the "Format" tab in the "Visualizations" pane on the right. click on General->Fill in the following values..
Colaberry Logo:
             Size: Height =70, Width=212
              Position: Horizontal=0, Vertical =0
Amazon Logo:
               Size: Height =70, Width=200
                position: Horizontal =1080, Width=0
