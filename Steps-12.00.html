Creating 'Refreshed Date' table 12

1. Open Power BI desktop and click on the Transform Data option.
2.  Under the Home ribbon, click on the New Source option and select Blank Query.
3. Once you have Blank Query table Query1 in place under the Queries section, right-click on it and rename it to Refreshed Date.
4. Enter the following formula into the function bar: "= DateTime.LocalNow ()".
5. Click "To Table" to convert the date data to a table format.
6. From the Transform menu, choose Change Data Type and select the Date/Time option.
