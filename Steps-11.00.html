Creating Calendar Table 11


1.    Go to Modeling Tab click on new table.
2.    Type the following DAX formula on the formula bar, click on the check mark and save.

Calendar = ADDCOLUMNS(
    CALENDAR(DATE(2011, 1, 1), DATE(2022, 12, 31)),
    "Year", YEAR([Date]),
    "Month", MONTH([Date]),
    "Month Name", FORMAT([Date], "MMMM"),
    "Quarter", QUARTER([Date]),
    "Week Number", WEEKNUM([Date], 2),  -- 2 indicates week starts on Monday
    "Day", DAY([Date]),
    "Day of Week", FORMAT([Date], "dddd"),
    "YearMonthShort", FORMAT([Date], "yyyy/mmm"),  -- Use lowercase "yyyy"
    "Day of Week Number", WEEKDAY([Date], 2)  -- 2 indicates Sunday is the first day of the week
)
