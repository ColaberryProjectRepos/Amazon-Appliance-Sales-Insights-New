List of Encountered Challenges in the Project:

1. Data Set Complexity: The initial challenge involved working with a data set containing unnecessary columns, which added complexity to the analysis process. The presence of extraneous information required careful data cleansing and restructuring to focus solely on relevant data.

2.KPI Calculation: Another significant challenge was calculating the Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) required for the project. This entailed deriving meaningful metrics from the available data, ensuring their accuracy and relevance. Creating formulas to compute KPIs added complexity, requiring careful validation to generate insightful results.

3.Chart Selection: Choosing the appropriate charts for visualizing the data was a key decision. Selecting charts that effectively conveyed trends and patterns in appliance sales was pivotal for accurate interpretation. Balancing clarity, aesthetics, and representation of the data's essence was an ongoing consideration.

4.Choosing the Right KPIs: Defining the relevant KPIs to measure the project's success was another challenge. Identifying the most pertinent KPIs that encapsulated crucial aspects of appliance sales, such as revenue, sales trends, and customer satisfaction, was crucial for deriving actionable insights.
