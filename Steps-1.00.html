Problem Statement 1

Between 2011 and 2022, Amazon faced declining appliance sales revenue in the global e-commerce landscape. Supply chain shifts, consumer behavior changes, and logistical complexities influenced its online retail experience. Despite 25% annual growth in net sales from 2015 to 2020, appliance revenue dropped by 10% ,showcasing online retail's complexity. Adapting to evolving consumer preferences and market fluctuations, Amazon aimed to refine strategies and maintain online retail dominance.
